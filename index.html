About Me

I'm a BTech Electronics and Communication Engineering student at Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT), with a deep interest in embedded systems, robotics, and web development. I'm passionate about building real-world tech solutions that combine hardware and software in practical, meaningful ways.

I have worked on various hands-on projects, including a mine rescue and detection robot powered by ESP32, sensors, and autonomous/manual modes. I'm also actively exploring web development, creating responsive websites and continuously learning front-end and back-end technologies.

As the Vice Chair of the IEEE APS CUSAT Student Branch Chapter, I help organize technical events and workshops, contributing to the student tech community.
